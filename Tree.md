🌳 THE GREAT TREE OF LIFE

Overview

The Great Tree of Life stands at the center of the world and serves as the starting location for every player. It is the oldest living thing in existence and can be seen from nearly every region of the map. The tree represents life, hope, and the connection between all kingdoms.

Players begin their adventure beneath its branches.

---

📏 Scale

The tree is designed to feel enormous while remaining practical for Roblox.

- Height: 140 studs
- Trunk Diameter: 40 studs
- Canopy Diameter: 120 studs
- Main Roots: 8–12 studs wide
- Spawn Platform Diameter: 40 studs

The tree should dominate the skyline and become the world's main landmark.

---

🌳 Trunk Design

The trunk should feel ancient and alive.

Appearance

- Massive twisted bark
- Deep vertical grooves
- Large knots and natural scars
- Moss growing between cracks
- Hanging vines
- Small mushrooms at the base

Colors

Bottom:

- Dark Brown
- Almost Black

Middle:

- Rich Oak Brown

Upper:

- Golden Brown

Avoid a perfect cylinder shape. Use multiple parts and curves to create a natural appearance.

---

✨ Life Energy Veins

Ancient life energy flows through the tree.

Appearance

- Thin glowing cracks in the bark
- Neon material
- Soft pulsing animation

Colors

- Emerald Green
- Cyan Blue
- Soft Gold

The glow should be subtle during the day and brighter at night.

---

🌿 The Canopy

The canopy is a giant living roof over the spawn area.

Features

- Large leaf clusters
- Hanging vines
- Dense branches
- Sunlight filtering through leaves

Effects

- Falling leaf particles
- Floating pollen particles
- Occasional drifting leaves

The canopy should feel magical and alive.

---

🌸 Eternal Blossoms

Ancient flowers grow throughout the tree.

Appearance

- Large glowing flowers
- White petals
- Pink petals
- Golden centers

Effects

- Gentle glow
- Floating particles
- Open wider during the night

These flowers act as natural lanterns after sunset.

---

🌊 Sacred Waters

Fresh water flows from hidden springs beneath the roots.

Features

- Circular stream around the tree
- Small waterfalls
- Lily pads
- Wooden bridges

Sounds

- Flowing water
- Gentle splashes

The water symbolizes life flowing from the tree.

---

🌳 Main Roots

Six giant roots spread across the world.

Each root acts as a natural road leading players toward future regions.

Root One

Forest Kingdom

Root Two

Starter Village

Root Three

Crystal Cave

Root Four

Training Grounds

Root Five

Future Expansion Area

Root Six

Boss Region

The roots help guide players naturally without needing arrows.

---

🏛 Spawn Platform

Players spawn on a circular stone platform surrounding the tree.

Features

- Ancient stone floor
- Moss-covered edges
- Golden runes
- Soft glowing symbols
- Central spawn point

Welcome Message

"Welcome, Traveler."

"Every journey begins beneath the Tree of Life."

---

Name

☀️ Day Vision

During the day the tree feels warm and welcoming.

Lighting

- Bright sunlight
- Soft shadows
- Golden light rays

Environment

- Butterflies
- Birds
- Floating pollen
- Sparkling water

The area should feel safe and full of life.

---

🌙 Night Vision

At night the Tree of Life transforms into something magical.

Tree Effects

- Life Energy Veins glow brighter
- Eternal Blossoms illuminate
- Fireflies appear
- Floating particles increase

Lighting

- Soft blue and green glow
- Moonlight through branches
- Magical atmosphere

Environment

- Fireflies around roots
- Gentle night breeze
- Glowing flowers
- Reflections on water

Players should stop and admire the tree when night arrives.

The Tree of Life becomes a giant natural beacon visible across the entire world.

Its glow reminds players that no matter how far they travel, they can always find their way home.
